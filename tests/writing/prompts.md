## Mini-Essay Prompt (A1–A2)
Write a short mini-essay (about 80–150 words) on the topic below.

Prompt:
"Describe your favorite place and explain why you like it."

Guidance:
- 80–150 words
- Clear topic sentence, 1–2 supporting sentences, and a concluding sentence
- Spelling and grammar expected at A1–A2 level
