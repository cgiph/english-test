## Mini-Essay Holistic Rubric (1–10) — CEFR equivalents

Score 10 — CEFR A2: Clear simple communication, correct sentences, adequate vocabulary for the topic.
9 — A2: Minor errors, clear organization.
8 — A2: Understandable, some omission or inaccuracies.
7 — A2-: Frequent small errors but meaning clear.
6 — A1+: Basic control, simple sentence use with some errors.
5 — A1+: Understandable with noticeable errors and limited vocabulary.
4 — A1: Very limited expression; frequent errors.
3 — Below A1: Very short and hard to understand.
2 — Below A1: Few intelligible words/phrases.
1 — Below A1: Not communicative.

Scoring notes:
- Examiner should provide a single holistic score (1–10) and optional short comments.
- The scoring script accepts a manual essay score input; essays are not automatically graded.
